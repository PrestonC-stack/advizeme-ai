Project Overview

AI-powered estimate-building and DVI analysis system for an automotive repair shop (Callahan Auto & Diesel) designed to convert technician findings into clean, sellable, accurate estimates and follow-up workflows.

Primary function: take DVIs, ROs, and estimates → standardize into TechMetric-ready job blocks, audit for completeness, enforce quality standards, and guide advisors on next actions.

Core Objective

Build a system that:

Converts technician findings into structured, estimate-ready job blocks
Ensures no missed revenue, no overcharging, no duplication
Enforces DVI quality and documentation standards
Produces advisor-ready estimates + follow-up plans
Enables scalable, repeatable, and auditable repair workflows
Business Context
Business: Callahan Auto & Diesel (Mesa, AZ)
Services: light-duty, diesel, fleet, RV repair
